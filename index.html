In the game where you have to plant a garden, called "Grow A Garden", there are many interesting things, and one of the most interesting is seeds. They come in different sizes. Some can cost 10 dollars, and some can cost 10 million. And also - Seeds - one of the most important for growing a garden.

Carrot - Carrots grow quite quickly, as they are a cheap seeds They can grow both small (most often) and large (rarely). It costs 10 Sheckles.

Strawberry - Strawberries also don't take much time to grow. They grow on branches and can be any size too. It costs 50 Sheckles.

Blueberry - Blueberries are a more expensive seeds, and automatically, grow a little longer. They grow on a bush, and like all other stocks, can be small or large in size. It costs 400 Sheckles.

Orange Tulip - The orange tulip is most likely not available. Therefore, it is slightly different from the first three seeds. It costs 600 Sheckles.

Tomato - Tomatoes are always in stock. And their size is a little more interesting, because tomatoes come in both small and large sizes. It costs 800 Sheckles.

Corn - Corn is a rarer seed. It grows like a flower, and it can look both small and large. It costs 1,400 Sheckles.

Daffodil - Daffodil - quite useful for new players, because they appear quite rarely in the stock. They are usually small in size, so in my opinion they are not very useful. It costs 1,000 Sheckles.

Watermelon - Watermelons are rarely sold, but if a more expensive one doesn't sell, a watermelon may be sold. It's usually a bit big, but it can also become even bigger than it is. It costs 2,500 Sheckles.

Pumpkin - Pumpkins appear much less frequently in stock, but they give more money. It costs 3,000 Sheckles.

Apple - Apples are very rare, even rarer than watermelons or pumpkins. But apples (like mangoes) can grow quite large. They also grow on trees. It costs 3,250 Sheckles.

Bamboo - Bamboo is quite useful because it grows tall, and if it is also wide and has a mutation, it will give a lot of money. It costs 3,250 Sheckles.

Coconut - Coconuts grow on large palm trees, and there are many of them on one tree. They, like everyone else, can be of different sizes. It costs 6,000 Sheckles.

Cactus - A cactus is a plant that is born from a larger cactus. It is usually small, but can come in different sizes anyway. It costs 15,000 Sheckles.

Dragon Fruit - A very interesting, colorful dragon fruit that grows on a tree. It comes in several sizes, like all of them. It costs 45,000 Sheckles.

Mango - As I mentioned in the case of the apple, mangoes can be big, even very big. And they also give you a lot of Sheckles. It costs 100,000 Sheckles.
